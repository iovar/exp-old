Earth is being attacked by space viruses send by the Xutr-Gaad alien race.

These viruses have been known to cause an epidemic known as Epidemic X or simply EpidemiX.

The first outbreaks have been contained but a continuous flow of viruses is thrown towards Earth, to prepare the ground for an invasion.

If humans are to survive and fight back, the Epidemix must be destroyed before it reaches the atmosphere...
